Community Guidelines

These simple expectations help keep NTChat respectful, useful, and enjoyable for everyone.

Be respectful

Engage with others in good faith. Personal attacks, harassment, and toxic behavior have no place here.

Stay on topic

Use room categories and tags properly so conversations remain organized and useful for members.

Share responsibly

Avoid spam, misleading content, or anything that would disrupt the room experience for others.

Respect moderation

Staff and room managers may step in when content breaks community expectations or platform rules.
